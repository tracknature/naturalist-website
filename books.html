Technique

Advanced tracking techniques and species-specific guides for serious trackers.

Tracking Wolves: The Basics

By Jim Halfpenny and Tracy Furman

Essential guide to understanding wolf footprints, trails, and sign. Find wolves by tracks, urine, scat, and carcasses. Includes comparative information for domestic dogs, coyotes, and other carnivores.

$9.95

Tracking Cougars

By Jim Halfpenny

Guide to identifying cougar footprints and distinguishing them from dogs and other animals. Covers footprints, gait, trails, scat, scent marking, kills, and burials.

$14.95

Tracking Canids

By Jim Halfpenny

For the hardcore tracker who wishes to study tracks from members of the wolf family in great detail: wolves, coyotes, foxes, and dogs. Advanced identification techniques.

$24.95

Track Plates for Mammals

By Jim Halfpenny, Meghan Walla-Murphy, Elizabeth Rogers, and David Tiller

A "How-To-Manual" and aid to footprint identification. Get tracks anywhere! Ingenious devices allow tracking where natural substrates don't permit it. Used for finding rare species and non-invasive censusing.

$19.95

Track Scene Investigation: Forensic Wildlife Tracking

By Jim Halfpenny

44 case analyses spanning investigations into rare and endangered species, animal depredation, and cougar, wolverine, and lynx sightings. Forensic tracking techniques applied to real-world scenarios.

$29.95
