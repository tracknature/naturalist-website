Our Mission

A Naturalist's World is dedicated to providing educational programs and materials about natural history and ecology. We believe in learning through direct field experience - observing, tracking, and understanding wildlife in their natural habitats.

Track Education Center & Museum

The home of A Naturalist's World in Gardiner, Montana - Gateway to Yellowstone National Park

Located in the Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem, the Track Education Center serves as both our headquarters and a museum preserving the natural history of the region.

Museum Collection

  • Original plaster casts of wild animal tracks from across North America
  • Archive of historic Yellowstone wolf and bear documentation

Diann Thompson, BSN

Co-Owner ‚Ä¢ Field Naturalist ‚Ä¢ Educator

Diann Thompson is co-owner of A Naturalist's World and Jim's partner in both life and field work. With a background in nursing (BSN), Diann brings a unique perspective to wildlife education, combining scientific rigor with compassionate teaching.

Diann co-authored "Discovering Yellowstone Wolves," the first book published about wolf restoration in Yellowstone, documenting the historic return of wolves to the park and the first 1.5 years of the restoration program.

Together, Jim and Diann have taught thousands of students the art and science of wildlife tracking, fostering a new generation of naturalists and conservationists.
