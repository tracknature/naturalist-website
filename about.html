Meet Dr. Jim Halfpenny

With over five decades of field experience, Dr. Jim Halfpenny is one of North America's leading experts in natural history, carnivore ecology, cold weather ecology and wildlife tracking education.

Based in Gardiner, Montana, at the doorstep of Yellowstone National Park, Jim has dedicated his life to understanding and teaching about the wildlife of the Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem.

  • Explorer: Polar regions including Greenland
  • Scientist: Director Mountain Research Station
  • Ecologist: Alpine, Winter & Long Term Ecology Research programs
  • Tracker: Owner of the world's largest forensic wildlife tracking operation
  • Author: 30+ tracking and wildlife books and videos
  • Educator: Over 50 years of field research and education
  • Veteran: U.S. Navy - Vietnam
