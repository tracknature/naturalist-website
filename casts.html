Footprint Casts

Life-size 3D replicas of wild animal tracks

All footprint casts are life-size from wild, free-roaming animals and come with a written history of the animal, cast surface and location. Each is an exact duplicate and measurements may be made from the cast.

Casts were selected for large size; they are not from average sized animals. They are impressive!

In the field, I locate excellent quality tracks in a fresh mud surface. Into these I pour plaster. The resulting cast is a positive of the foot of the animal. Polylactic casts are then created by making a 3D digital file and printing the file on a 3D printer to make a cast. The cast is a thing of beauty both in the natural history it shows and simply as art.

All casts were made by Dr. Jim Halfpenny.
