30 Years of Yellowstone Wolves

By Leo Leckie, Jim Halfpenny, and Shauna Baron

Celebrate 30 years of wolf restoration! Learn life histories and genealogy of 22 famous and favorite wolves and 18 current alphas. Ancestries and stories that bring the Yellowstone wolf saga to life.

Charting Yellowstone Wolves: 25th Anniversary

By Jim Halfpenny

The first 25 years of wolf restoration in the Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem. Includes all wolf charts since the beginning and biographies for radio collared wolves, plus instructions on accessing wolf genealogy.

$24.95

Wolves

Yellowstone Wolves in the Wild

By Jim Halfpenny

Everything you wanted to know about the wolves of Yellowstone! Comprehensive coverage of wolf restoration, pack dynamics, and behavior in the Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem.

$19.95

Discovering Yellowstone Wolves

By Jim Halfpenny and Diann Thompson

Historic first edition documenting wolf restoration. Pictures of original wolves, their pack history in Canada, and details of the first 1.5 years. A must-have historical glimpse.

$19.95

Bears

Yellowstone Bears in the Wild

By Jim Halfpenny

The comprehensive portrait of grizzly and black bears in the Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem. Includes cutting-edge research on bear biology, ecology, denning, hibernation, and responses to ecological changes.

$19.95

Rare Mammal Moments of the Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem

By Jim Halfpenny

The rarest biological, behavioral, and ecological events of the secret lives of mammals captured by the photographer. Stunning documentation of moments few ever witness.

Tracking & Identification

Tracking Wolves: The Basics

By Jim Halfpenny and Tracy Furman

Essential guide to understanding wolf footprints, trails, and sign. Find wolves by tracks, urine, scat, and carcasses. Includes comparative information for domestic dogs, coyotes, and other carnivores.

$9.95

Track Plates for Mammals

By Jim Halfpenny, Meghan Walla-Murphy, Elizabeth Rogers, and David Tiller

A "How-To-Manual" and aid to footprint identification. Get tracks anywhere! Ingenious devices allow tracking where natural substrates don't permit it. Used for finding rare species and non-invasive censusing.

$19.95

Scats and Tracks of North America

By Jim Halfpenny, illustrated by Todd Tealander

Coast-to-coast guide to tracking what critters went before you. Nearly 150 species of amphibians, reptiles, birds, and mammals. Easy reference descriptions, measurements, and illustrations.

$16.95

Tracking Cougars

Reprint of the original guide to identifying cougar footprints and distinguishing them from dogs and other animals. Covers footprints, gait, trails, scat, scent marking, kills, and burials.

Tracking Canids

By Jim Halfpenny

For the hardcore tracker who wishes to study tracks from members of the wolf family in great detail: wolves, coyotes, foxes, and dogs. Advanced identification techniques.

$24.95
