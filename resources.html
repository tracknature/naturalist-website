Yellowstone Wolf Family Tree

Explore 30 years of wolf genealogy in the Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem

Access the complete genealogical database of Yellowstone's wolf packs from 1995 to present. This comprehensive family tree documents:

  • Every radio-collared wolf and their lineage
  • Pack formations, splits, and territories
  • Famous wolves and their descendants (21M, 06, 755M, 832F, and more)
  • Breeding pairs and pup survival rates
  • Migration patterns between packs
  • 30 years of restoration history

The database is hosted on Ancestry.com and provides an interactive way to explore how wolf packs are related and how the population has evolved over three decades.

Access Wolf Family Tree Learn More in Our Books

Note: Instructions for accessing the Yellowstone Wolf Genealogy are included in the book "Charting Yellowstone Wolves: 25th Anniversary"

Scientific Publications

Dr. Halfpenny has published over 100 scientific articles in peer-reviewed journals covering topics including:

  • Wildlife tracking methodology and forensic techniques
  • Carnivore ecology and behavior in the Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem
  • Alpine and winter ecology research
  • Wolf restoration and population dynamics
  • Grizzly and black bear biology and conservation
  • Long-term ecological monitoring and climate impacts
